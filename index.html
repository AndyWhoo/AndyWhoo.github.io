Sometimes, you have to get knocked down lower than you have ever been to stand back up taller than you were.

Artist Statement

My work focuses on the deviation from daily routine and the abstraction of normal everyday life. People follow routine which gives our lives a sense of cohesion and structure, I have a routine which keeps me sane. However, I believe that a little disruption is needed every once in a while, a different point of view as opposed to the same old sights and sounds we experience day after day.I tend to take what is real, and I will bend it until the shape is physically unrecognizable. I will dabble in any medium from digital to pen and pencil, whatever I am feeling at the current time, I will utilize.
