Experimental Video

This short clip was a culmination of a few weeks of nightmares, or uncomfortable visions I had during a stressful period in my life. It is the image of a cow created with the distorted soundwaves of a cow's moo. It doesn't last long, as with the actual nightmares I had. An Adobe Photoshop piece plus Audition.
